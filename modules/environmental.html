Hypothermia: Cold Weather Emergency

Definition: Hypothermia occurs when body temperature drops below 95째F (35째C).
Signs of Hypothermia
  • Shivering
  • Slurred speech
  • Slow, shallow breathing
  • Weak pulse
  • Confusion or drowsiness
First Aid Steps
  1. Move person to warm, dry area
  2. Remove wet clothing
  3. Warm core body first (chest, neck, head)
  4. Use warm blankets or body heat
  5. Seek medical attention

Heat Stroke: Extreme Heat Emergency

Warning: Heat stroke can be life-threatening and requires immediate action.
Heat Stroke Symptoms
  • High body temperature (104째F/40째C or higher)
  • Hot, red, dry skin
  • Rapid, strong pulse
  • Throbbing headache
  • Dizziness or confusion
Emergency Response
  1. Call emergency services
  2. Move to cool area
  3. Remove excess clothing
  4. Cool with wet towels or ice packs
  5. Do not give fluids if unconscious

Frostbite: Freezing Tissue Damage

Caution: Frostbite can cause permanent tissue damage.
Frostbite Stages
  • Skin turns white or grayish-yellow
  • Skin feels hard or waxy
  • Numbness in affected area
  • Blistering in severe cases
Treatment Steps
  1. Get to warm area immediately
  2. Remove wet or tight clothing
  3. Warm affected area gradually
  4. Do not rub or massage area
  5. Seek medical help

Lightning Strikes: Electrical Emergency

Immediate Action: Lightning strikes can be fatal.
+
Lightning Strike Effects
  • Cardiac arrest
  • Burns
  • Neurological damage
  • Muscle and nerve injuries
Immediate Response
  1. Ensure scene is safe
  2. Call emergency services
  3. Check for breathing and pulse
  4. Begin CPR if necessary
  5. Treat for shock

Drowning Prevention and Response

Prevention is Key: Most drowning incidents are preventable.
Drowning Prevention
  • Learn to swim
  • Always supervise children
  • Use life jackets
  • Avoid alcohol near water
  • Know CPR
Drowning Response
  1. Remove from water safely
  2. Check for responsiveness
  3. Begin CPR if not breathing
  4. Call emergency services
  5. Continue CPR until help arrives
